image via Pixabay user 'Suju" (CC0 Creative Commons) | link to image

  • On September 17, 2017, “Grace” went on a date with Aziz Ansari. On January 13, 2018, babe.net published Grace’s story.
  • In the following days, news outlets, blog writers, and social media users engaged in a public converation about Grace's description of the night and the efficacy of the babe.net article in retelling her story.
  • This website seeks to provide a context within which an interested party can begin to investigate the various arguments regarding:
    • the merits of Grace's claims and
    • the merits of the the babe.net article
